For Wholesale Buyers

As the popularity of Dinara Kasko brand continues to increase worldwide, we are actively seeking Distributors around the world to meet this demand. Since our start in 2015 we have supplied products directly to customers to all 4 corners of the globe.

What the requirements?

01

Local Market knowledge

Distributors should have a thorough knowledge and understanding of the local market in their designated region. This includes insights into consumer preferences, market trends, and regulatory requirements. Knowledge of the local market is essential for successful promotion and distribution of Dinara Kasko products.

02

Establish Presence

Distributors must have an existing platform, such as website, physical store, or both or the capability to build up such an audience quickly and effectively, is crucial for maximizing the brand`s visibility and sales.

03

National Coverage

Distributors should have the capacity to cover the entire country and target different market segments. This involves having a strategic plan to reach customers in various regions, ensuring that Dinara Kasko products are accessible to a wide audience.

04

Financial Capacity

Distributors must demonstrate the financial capacity to maintain sufficient stock levels of Dinara Kasko products. This ensures a consistent supply to meet customer demand and prevents stockouts. Financial stability is key to sustaining a long-term and successful partnership.
