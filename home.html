dinara kasko

Dinara Kasko Pastry Art was founded in 2016 by the talented young designer Dinara Kasko.


Dinara Kasko became famous for her unusual cakes, which are created using various modern innovative technologies, 3D modeling programs, as well as printing on a 3D printer. Thanks to her architectural past and her new take on confectionery design, her cakes have generated an unprecedented buzz and went viral on the web. Geometric confectionery proved to be very popular with both regular social media users and confectioners who wanted to create the same cakes for their customers.

recipes

Each of our molds comes with a unique, detailed, and incredibly delicious recipe for a cakes. All recipes are carefully tested and perfected, so you won’t have to worry about what to put inside — everything is already thoughtfully crafted for you

With our molds, you don’t need to add extra decorations. Simply remove the cake from the mold, cover it with glaze or spray, and it’s your masterpiece.

But of course, the choice is always yours. Our design is like a blank canvas, allowing you to create any masterpiece using your imagination.

faq

What is the difference between a factory-made mold and a handmade mold?
There is no difference for use — both factory-made and handmade molds are equally suitable for desserts. The main thing is to choose a design you like! 😊

Handmade molds are usually made of white or translucent silicone, packed in kraft cardboard, and come with a QR code to download a recipe in PDF format.

Factory-made molds are made of gray silicone and come in a white branded box with a printed recipe booklet.
