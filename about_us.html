dinara kasko

Dinara Kasko Pastry Art was founded in 2016 by the talented young designer Dinara Kasko. Dinara Kasko became famous for her unusual cakes, which are created using various modern innovative technologies, 3D modeling programs, as well as printing on a 3D printer. Thanks to her architectural past and her new take on confectionery design, her cakes have generated an unprecedented buzz and went viral on the web. Geometric confectionery proved to be very popular with both regular social media users and confectioners who wanted to create the same cakes for their customers.

about my process

We use 3D printing and silicone to bring any design to life, creating over 200 unique molds in various sizes. Our printers and materials have powered countless hours of innovation.

Concept & Design

Start with a creative idea and use 3D modeling software to design the mold.

3D Printing

Bring the design to life by 3D printing a precise plastic model.

Silicone Casting

Prepare and pour silicone into the 3D-printed model to create the mold.

Curing & Finishing:

Allow the silicone to cure, then carefully remove the mold for final touches.

packing

We print detailed recipes and ensure every mold comes in high-quality, functional packaging.

Ready for Use

The finished silicone mold is ready to help create stunning, professional cake designs.
